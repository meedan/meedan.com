This is achieved through training, software development, programming and research with a global network of organisations based across four main areas of focus: North Africa Western Asia, Latin America, Sub-Saharan Africa and the APAC region.

{% include icon_indepth.html %}

Our work includes

01
Partner Collaboration
We work with global partners, strengthening their capacity to: counter misinformation and disinformation; provide credible information and address inequalities in access; develop media literacy skills so they, and the communities they work with, are better equipped to hold public figures to account.
→
02
Network
Our Network brings together civil society organisations, independent media collectives and grassroot activist groups and individuals working at the intersection of media and technology. We provide open-access tools and networking opportunities for communities facing historic and systemic barriers in these fields.
→
03
Microgrants
We provide funding opportunities for organisations, collectives, journalists and activists as a contribution to their ongoing efforts for advocacy and sustainability. These are often in response to particular themes or crises, such as Covid-19 pandemic and the 2019 Beirut explosion.
→
